WORLD CUP 2026
RULES

The player with the most points at the end of the three rounds will be crowned the champion. Don't worry if you miss a round - there are prizes avaliable for the winners of the individual rounds.

ROUND 1

TEAM PICKS

Pick the 12 teams you think will finish second in the group stage and the 8 teams you think will qualify as the best 3rd placed teams. For every team you pick who qualifies for the last 32 you win 2 points. For every team you place correctly you also win 2 points. Therefore, if you correctly predict a team will qualify in 2nd or 3rd place you will win 4 points. You will win 2 points if a chosen team qualifies but not in the position you predicted. You will win 2 points if you correctly predict a team to finish 3rd but they do not qualify.

PLAYER PICKS

Pick one combo of players from the pre-set list. You will get 2 points for each goal scored by those players in the group stage (subject to a combined cap of 20 points).

TIEBREAKER

Select the total number of goals you think will be scored in the group stage. This will determine the winner of the round if two or more players are tied on points.

ROUND 2

TEAM PICKS

Predict the winner of each match in the last 32, last 16 and quarter finals. Predict whether that team will win the match in normal time, in extra time or on penalties! You win 2 points for correctly predicting the winner of a match and a bonus point if you correctly predict whether they do it in normal time, in extra time or on penalties. In the last 16 and quarter finals, you do not need your predicted winner to beat the team that you predicted they would be playing - you will still earn points if they beat a different team.

PLAYER PICKS

Pick one combo of players from the pre-set list. You will get 2 points for each goal scored by those players in the group stage (subject to a combined cap of 16 points).

TIEBREAKER

Select the total number of goals you think will be scored in the last 32, last 16 and quarters (not including penalties in shootouts). This will determine the winner of the round if two or more players are tied on points.
